Biography.

I grew up in the Novi area and went to Detroit Catholic Central for High School, Michigan State University for a Bachelor's in TV/Film Production, and worked for two years as a mechnical engineer before I became interested in programming.

I interned at Quicken Loans in their Technology department and became quickly intrigued with the programming that they were doing. They suggested Grand Circus and so here I am.
